🔑 How the system scores picks — table key (click to expand)
Column glossary
  • Tier — final confidence bucket from risk-adjusted edge. PREMIUM > CORE > SECONDARY > WATCH (logged, not played).
  • Odds / Line — locked at noon ET. If FanDuel's line moved between scout (9 AM) and lock, picks are re-scored against the current FD price (we never bet a stale number).
  • Stake — Tier 1 = 1u = $3 LIVE / $5 PAPER. Daily cap 5u = $15 LIVE.
  • Edge pp — model hit-rate minus implied probability of FD odds, in percentage points. 5pp+ is required for action.
  • RAE (risk-adjusted edge) — edge tilted by confidence + variance + factor stack. Drives tier assignment.
  • Strategy — which active playbook the pick belongs to (see Strategies tab).
  • Result — Pending → Won / Lost / Push after settlement.
11-factor scoring stack (multiplied into combined_factor → RAE)
1. Statcast — xBA, barrel%, hard-hit % (L30)
2. Park factor — venue HR / runs factor
3. Handedness — batter vs L/R pitcher splits
4. Pitcher matchup — recent SP form (last 5 starts)
5. Weather — temp, wind, roof state
6. Last-10 — recent player performance
7. Team form — last-10 team run-scoring
8. Player performer — season-long hit-rate baseline
9. BvP — career batter-vs-pitcher (≥10 PA gates in)
10. Umpire — plate ump K%/runs bias
11. Bullpen — opposing pen rest / fatigue index
NRFI v2 (Today tab "Top NRFI Plays" only)
Pitcher 1st-inning scoreless-rate (weighted 0.65) × team 1st-inning offensive scoreless-rate (0.35), per half-inning, shrunk toward 0.73 prior when sample < 10 starts. Adjusted by park-runs factor and game-time temp. ≥60% is the bettable floor; min_sample ≥ 5 required for confidence.
Hover any pick row or NRFI row for the full per-factor breakdown. Math cannot override research; research cannot override math.
